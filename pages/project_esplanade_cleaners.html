Esplanade Cleaners is a local dry cleaning and alterations company in the heart of the Esplanade, Toronto.

This tiny shop has been a part of the Esplanade community for over 25 years, and when the owners came to me to build their website, they were very enthusiastic about creating an image for the business which would represent their loyalty to the esplanade community, as well as their services tailored the areas population demographic.

One thing that I felt was important to display on the website was a collection of the clients Google Reviews. To achieve this I had to build my own custom 'carousel-style' comments section which made use of the Google Maps API.

What the mini-application does is retreive all the google reviews for esplanade cleaners using the owners 'business' ID (supplied by Google), and then parses the response into a readable javascript object - which is then loaded into the websites display. Have a look at the code HERE.
