Polaris Systems Inc. is a medium-sized business which focuses on large-scale packaging solutions for various manufacturing industries. Using Wordpress, I developed a custom theme as well as a custom database in order to complement the specific needs of the company. This included the development of several logic loops to handle the custom database queries necessary for the company's wide variety of products and services. The major technologies used for this project are Wordpress, Bootstrap, PHP, MySQL, JavaScript, jQuery, and of course HTML and CSS.

A major component to the structure of this website resides in the "Custom-Post-Types" feature within the Wordpress framework. Using custom-post-types I was able to organize all the companies products into a variety of categories, allowing me to add filtering for customers looking for a specific type of product. For example, each product can be filtered by brand, type of product, product performance, as well as a variety of other categories.

Using Custom-Post-Types also allowed me to scale the website approprietly when adding more products to the database, as well as preparing it for the future ability to sell the products online.
